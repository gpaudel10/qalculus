# Progress and public claims

**Last updated:** 29 June 2026

This page keeps our public language honest. It is intentionally less detailed
than an internal engineering record: it shows maturity without exposing the
private implementation or product-sensitive ideas.

## Current position

| Area | Public status | What that means today |
|---|---|---|
| Qalculus Labs public foundation | In development | The public repository, website direction, policies, and communication are being established. |
| Qalculus Data Systems foundation | Implemented | An early private engineering foundation exists. This is not a production-readiness claim. |
| Limited end-to-end validation | Verified | One bounded internal path has been exercised with synthetic data. Details remain private. |
| Broader product capability | In development | Reliability, coverage, reviewability, and deployment practices still need substantial work. |
| Early collaborator evaluation | Planned | The evaluation approach is defined, but no open pilot programme is announced. |
| Next-generation product | Planned | Direction-setting work will follow lessons from the current platform. |
| Future AI systems | Research | This is a separate research direction, not a released product. |
| Mathematical research | Research | Public descriptions remain at domain level until specific work is ready to publish. |

## Claims we do not make today

We do not currently claim:

- production readiness or a live customer deployment;
- independent security certification or regulatory approval;
- guaranteed data locality in every environment;
- universal correctness, proof, speed, or cost improvement;
- a released AI infrastructure product; or
- an advantage in quantum or other emerging computation.

## When a status changes

Stronger wording should follow stronger evidence. Before updating a public
status, we should record internally what was tested, when it was tested, the
environment and input class, the baseline where relevant, the observed result,
and the limitations that remain.
